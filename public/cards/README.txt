# Card images (placeholders)

Each product in `src/data/products.js` points at a PNG in this folder (for example `/cards/itachi.png`).

**Replace these tiny placeholder files** with your real card scans or exports. Keep the same filenames, or update the `image` field in `src/data/products.js` to match your files.

Suggested dimensions for production: at least **800×1000** (portrait) for crisp grids and detail pages.
